# Globex Corp Employee Handbook

## 1. Work From Home Policy
Employees are allowed to work from home up to 2 days a week. The remaining 3 days must be spent in the office. 
However, there is an exception for the Engineering team: Engineers are allowed to work fully remote, 5 days a week, provided they attend the quarterly on-site sync.

## 2. Equipment Allowance
All new hires receive a $1000 equipment stipend for home office setup. This stipend must be expensed within the first 60 days of employment.

## 3. Vacation Time
Standard vacation policy is 15 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per year. Unused days do not roll over to the next year.
